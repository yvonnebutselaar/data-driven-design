This is a fictional data set where you have to predict money spent on Bol.com
The data set has been artificially constructed to include some relations between variables and be optimal for didactic purposes; however, because of this artificiality, many aspects of it will be unrealistic.

There are 2 data sets, one with hold-out data (target removed) that you need to predict.

Variables:

id:			id
gender:			gender
income:			income in euro
distance_center:	distance to a city center in km
website_visits:		website visits to bol.com per year
pages_viewed:		pages viewed on bol.com per year
time_social_media:	time spent on social in minutes per day
spent_bol:		amount spent on bol.com per year
